Holistic Evaluation of ASD in Women (HER-D)

Holistic Evaluation of ASD in Women (HER-D) is designed to identify behaviors and abilities in women (18+) that are associated with autism.

Approximate time: 5-7 minutes to respond to 25 questions.
Read each question and statement carefully, and rate how strongly you agree or disagree with it.
All responses are confidential.
